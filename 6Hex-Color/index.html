Understanding the Hex Color System

The Hex color model is a way of specifying colors using hexadecimal values. Each color is represented by a six-digit combination of numbers and letters defined by its mix of red, green, and blue (RGB). The format is #RRGGBB.

Basic Hex Colors

Red #FF0000
Green #00FF00
Blue #0000FF

Combination of Hex Colors

Yellow #FFFF00
Cyan #00FFFF
Magenta #FF00FF
White #FFFFFF
Black #000000
